Welcome to Marina's house

My personal universe of colors, chaos, and emotion. Here, I create art that breaks the rules and shows the beauty in being weird, raw, and human. Cyber Surrealism, Neo-Expressionism, and pure feeling. You're invited to explore, get lost, and if you want, create something together.

About me

I'm Marina and this is my house. Not with walls and windows, but with color, distortion, feeling, and chaos. My art lives somewhere between Cyber Surrealism and Neo-Expressionism, two worlds where emotions glitch and beauty breaks the rules. I started creating when I was 15 years old. Back then, I was making edits of the artist Tinashe, just for fun, until she saw my work, shared it, and said she was a fan. That moment unlocked something in me. Since then, I've been building this house, room by room, artwork by artwork. My style is messy on purpose. Weird on purpose. I don't believe in clean lines or making things look "normal," because we aren't normal. I believe art should be as unpredictable as we are inside, layered, strange, alive. So... welcome in. Take your shoes off. Stay as long as you want.
